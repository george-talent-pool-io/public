The Failure of Imagination

Most organizations treat AI merely as a coding accelerator. This is a failure of imagination. 62% of companies are stuck in the "Experimenting" phase because they haven't re-architected their workflow.

You cannot solve this by typing faster. You must transition from simply using AI to building an AI-Native SDLC.
